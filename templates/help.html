Help & FAQ

How do I book a room?

Login or register an account, browse available hotels from the Dashboard, click "View Rooms" on a hotel, then click "Book Now" on any available room.

How do I cancel a booking?

Go to "My Bookings" from the navigation bar and click "Cancel Booking" on any active reservation.

I forgot my password

Please contact the admin at admin@hotelhub.com to reset your password.

How do I contact support?

Visit the Contact Us page for our contact details.

Is my data safe?

Yes! All passwords are encrypted using industry-standard bcrypt hashing. Your data is stored securely.
