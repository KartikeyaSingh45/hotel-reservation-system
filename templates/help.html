Help & FAQ

How do I book a room?

1. Login or Register an account.
2. Go to the Dashboard and browse available hotels.
3. Click "View Rooms" on a hotel, then click "Book Now" on an available room.

How do I cancel a booking?

Go to "My Bookings" from the dashboard and click "Cancel Booking" on any active booking.

I forgot my password

Please contact the admin at admin@hotel.com to reset your password.
